About Us

Gianni's offers traditional, homemade Italian dishes to the Pape and Danforth area. Using authentic recipes and the finest of ingredients, we know that flavour is key! Try ordering from Gianni's today! Not sure what to order? Check out the Fried Calamari Dinner, the Chicken Souvlaki, or grab a bite of the Homemade Spanakopita!
